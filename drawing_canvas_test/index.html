Welcome to the time capsule for collecting people’s memories of dreams. Participants from all over the world are able to add on to this project by submitting their most memorable dreams to be interpreted by others. Either as a contributor or viewer, anyone can take part and experience the ever growing digitized collection of the world’s most unforgettable memories.
